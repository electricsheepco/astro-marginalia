# About Astro Marginalia

Astro Marginalia is a literary theme for small, curated knowledge projects: glossaries, dictionaries, field guides, annotated archives, cultural notebooks, and research indexes.

It is built around one idea: an entry should feel like both a beautiful page and a reliable record. The display form gets the hero treatment. The essay carries the voice. Marginalia, timelines, related links, and sources keep the argument inspectable.

The sample content is intentionally replaceable. Use the structure, not the demo claims.
